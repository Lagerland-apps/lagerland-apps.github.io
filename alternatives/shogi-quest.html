Questions

FAQ

What is the best ShogiQuest alternative for improving?
Shogiful — it's built for improvement rather than volume. Every game is analyzed on-device by YaneuraOu, every significant mistake is explained in plain language, and training is generated from your own weaknesses. Fully offline, no account, on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
How is Shogiful different from ShogiQuest?
ShogiQuest is a quick-play arena: tap, get matched, play, repeat. Shogiful answers the question the arena can't: why you lost — with a move-by-move review, plain-language explanations, and a training queue that drills those exact patterns.
Does Shogiful work offline?
Yes — completely. Engine, analysis, coaching, puzzles, and all 10 AI opponents run on your device. ShogiQuest needs a connection and an opponent; Shogiful works on a flight.
Can I play without knowing kanji?
Yes. The Western piece set uses symbolic icons with movement indicators, with a piece guide one tap away and progressive kanji introduction as you train.
How much does Shogiful cost compared to ShogiQuest?
Shogiful is free (all opponents, 2 analyses/day, 3 puzzles/day, no ads); Premium is $1.99/month, $9.99/year, or $19.99 lifetime. ShogiQuest is free with ads and optional purchases.
Can I use both ShogiQuest and Shogiful?
Yes — get fast human games on ShogiQuest, then use Shogiful to understand the recurring mistakes and train them away before going back online.
