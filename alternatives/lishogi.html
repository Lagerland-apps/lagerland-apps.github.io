How does Shogiful compare to Lishogi?

A platform and a coach. Here's where each one fits.

What it is
Shogiful: Native single-player training app — play AI opponents, get coached, train your weaknesses.

Lishogi: Free open-source web platform — online matchmaking, server analysis, puzzles, tournaments.
Native app vs browser
Shogiful: Native on iPhone, iPad, and Mac — one Universal Purchase, Mac keyboard shortcuts, no browser tab.

Lishogi: Web only; no official native iOS app.
Analysis & coaching
Shogiful: On-device YaneuraOu plus a coaching layer — mistakes classified into shogi-specific categories and explained in sentences.

Lishogi: Server-side engine analysis with an evaluation graph — you interpret it yourself.
Offline support
Shogiful: Fully offline — engine, puzzles, opponents, training all on-device.

Lishogi: Requires a connection for play and analysis.
Kanji barrier
Shogiful: Western piece sets with movement indicators, progressive kanji introduction, piece guide in every game.

Lishogi: Western-friendly notation and piece-set options too — genuinely good for non-Japanese players.
Pricing
Shogiful: Free tier; Premium $1.99/mo, $9.99/yr, or $19.99 lifetime.

Lishogi: Entirely free, donation-funded, no premium tier at all.

Questions

FAQ

Is there a native iPhone or Mac app like Lishogi?
Lishogi is a web platform without an official native iOS app. Shogiful is the closest native equivalent for the training side: a native iPhone, iPad, and Mac app with on-device YaneuraOu analysis, plain-language mistake explanations, tsume puzzles, and adaptive training — fully offline, no account.
How is Shogiful different from Lishogi?
Lishogi is a platform — online play, server analysis, puzzles, tournaments, free and open-source. Shogiful is a coach — it analyzes every game, classifies your mistakes into shogi-specific categories, explains each one in sentences, and builds a spaced-repetition training queue from your actual weaknesses.
Is Shogiful free like Lishogi?
Shogiful has a permanent free tier (all opponents, 2 analyses/day, 3 puzzles/day, no ads); Premium is $1.99/month, $9.99/year, or $19.99 lifetime. Lishogi is entirely free and donation-funded — if cost is the only criterion, Lishogi wins. Shogiful's case is the native offline experience and the coaching layer.
Can I play shogi offline with Shogiful?
Yes — completely. The engine, analysis, coaching, puzzles, and all 10 AI opponents run on your device. Lishogi requires a connection; Shogiful keeps working on a flight.
Does Shogiful have puzzles like Lishogi?
Yes — a verified tsume-shogi library scheduled with spaced repetition, mixed with exercises generated from the mistakes in your own games. An expanded ~500-problem library is on the public roadmap.
Can I use both Lishogi and Shogiful?
Yes — play humans on Lishogi, train offline in Shogiful. KIF import, which would let you bring Lishogi games into Shogiful's analyzer, is under consideration on the roadmap.
