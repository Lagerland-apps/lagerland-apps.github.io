FAQ

What is the best Compress Videos & Resize Video alternative?
MediaKit — the closest alternative if you want more than just video compression. Compress Videos & Resize Video is a popular, focused iPhone video compressor; MediaKit does that same on-device video compression and extends to 152 tools that also cover audio, image, PDF, and archive work, in one app, with a Universal Purchase across iPhone and Mac.
Does MediaKit do everything Compress Videos & Resize Video does?
For video compression, yes — MediaKit compresses and converts MP4, MOV, HEVC, and more on-device, with 21 export presets. Compress Videos & Resize Video is a simple, well-rated single-purpose tool; if shrinking clips is all you need, it does the job cleanly. MediaKit's advantage is breadth, adding audio, image, PDF, and archive tools.
Is MediaKit on-device like Compress Videos & Resize Video?
Yes — both process video on your device rather than uploading it. MediaKit is engineered across all 152 tools to avoid upload-to-a-website workflows, using Apple frameworks like AVFoundation.
How does MediaKit's pricing compare to Compress Videos & Resize Video?
MediaKit is free to start (Video Compress is free forever); Pro is $3.99/month, $22.99/year, or $39.99 lifetime. Compress Videos & Resize Video is free with IAP; its Pro removes ads and unlocks batch/unlimited compression, around $4.99/month, $5.99/year, or $12.99 lifetime. Just-video and want the lowest one-time price? Its lifetime is compelling. Want audio, image, PDF, and archives too? MediaKit's breadth wins.
Why pick MediaKit over Compress Videos & Resize Video?
Breadth, no ads, and one purchase across devices — compress a clip, convert a HEIC, extract audio, compress a PDF, and unzip an archive in one app, on both iPhone and Mac. Compress Videos & Resize Video is a solid, focused choice for shrinking clips but stays in the video lane.
Should I just use Compress Videos & Resize Video if I only compress video?
If video compression is genuinely all you need and you want the lowest one-time price, Compress Videos & Resize Video is a fair, popular pick (4.7 stars, 17,000+ ratings) and processes on-device. MediaKit's Video Compress is free forever too, but MediaKit earns its keep when you also work with audio, images, PDFs, or archives.
