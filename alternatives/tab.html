How does RightSplit compare to Tab?

Same workflow, more documented math. Here's the line.

RightSplit: Apple's on-device Vision framework (VNRecognizeTextRequest), 18+ languages, image never leaves the phone.

Tab: Photo-tap workflow; OCR engine not publicly documented.

RightSplit: Tip distributed proportionally by each person's subtotal share — so the $14-salad eater pays less tip than the $48-steak eater. Fair rounding documented openly.

Tab: Standard tax + tip calculation; per-person tip distribution not publicly documented.

FAQ

What is the best Tab alternative for iPhone?
RightSplit is a privacy-first iPhone alternative to Tab. It scans receipts on-device using Apple's Vision framework, splits item-by-item with shared dishes and proportional tip, and shares each person's total via iMessage or WhatsApp. Free; Pro is $1.99/year or $7.99 lifetime.
Is RightSplit free like Tab?
Yes — RightSplit's free tier covers equal splits and the tip calculator with no card on file. Item-based splitting (the core feature most people want a bill splitter for) is the Pro upgrade at $1.99/year or $7.99 lifetime. No subscription required, no auto-renew on the lifetime tier.
Does RightSplit upload receipt photos like some scan-based apps?
No. Receipt OCR runs entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework (VNRecognizeTextRequest). The image and parsed items never leave your phone — there are no servers, no cloud storage, no third-party analytics.
