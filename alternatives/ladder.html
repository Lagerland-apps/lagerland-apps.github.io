How does LiftLog compare to Ladder?

Different products, different lifters. Here's the line.

Coaching
LiftLog: Self-coached, periodized templates (PPL, Upper/Lower, 5/3/1, DUP, block, wave) + custom.

Ladder: Team-based programs led by named coaches with video workouts.
Pricing
LiftLog: Free 7-day trial (no card), then pay-what-you-can one-time $9.99–$39.99.

Ladder: Monthly / annual subscription per team or coach.
Format
LiftLog: Numbers-first log — tabular weights/reps, haptic-confirmed sets, 1RM analytics, PR moments.

Ladder: Video-led sessions with coach commentary and program structure.
Privacy / account
LiftLog: No account, no third-party tracking, data on device + optional iCloud sync.

Ladder: Account required; program data on Ladder's servers.
