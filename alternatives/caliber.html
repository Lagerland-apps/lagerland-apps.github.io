How does LiftLog compare to Caliber?

Two very different products. Here's what you're choosing between.

Coaching model
LiftLog: Self-coached, periodized templates. Linear, DUP, block, wave + auto deload.

Caliber: 1:1 remote human coach with custom programming and weekly check-ins.
Pricing
LiftLog: Free 7-day trial (no card), then pay-what-you-can one-time $9.99–$39.99.

Caliber: Monthly coaching subscription (varies by coach tier).
Privacy
LiftLog: No account, no third-party tracking, data on device + optional iCloud sync to your own Apple ID.

Caliber: Account required; data shared with assigned coach.
Best for
LiftLog: Lifters who already know the program they want to run and value craft + ownership.

Caliber: Lifters who want accountability and human eyes on every program decision.

Who should pick LiftLog vs Caliber?

Pick LiftLog if…
  • You already know which program you want to run (5/3/1, PPL, Upper/Lower, DUP, your own template)
  • You're tired of recurring fitness subscriptions and want to pay once
  • You'd rather a clean log than a coaching DM thread
  • You don't need accountability — you need a tool that gets out of the way during a working set
  • You want your training history to stay on your device, not a coach's server
Stick with Caliber if…
  • You actively want a remote human coach reviewing your training
  • You need someone telling you what to do this week, not a template
  • You value weekly check-ins and form-video feedback
  • You're a new lifter who'd benefit from outside structure
