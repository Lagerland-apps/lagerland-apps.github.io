What is the best Bobby alternative for iPhone?
AllPaid is the closest privacy-first alternative for people who want to track bills as well as subscriptions. Bobby is an excellent on-device subscription tracker with no bank login — AllPaid keeps that model and widens it to cover one-off bills, due dates, and subscriptions together, with a calendar, receipt detection, widgets, and Siri.
How is AllPaid different from Bobby?
Bobby is focused on recurring subscriptions as a grid of logo cards. AllPaid tracks subscriptions and one-time bills and due dates in one place, on a calendar and in widgets, and can detect a bill from a receipt. If you only track subscriptions, Bobby's focus is a feature; if you also juggle rent, insurance, and irregular bills, AllPaid is built for that.
Is AllPaid as private as Bobby?
Yes. Both avoid bank aggregation entirely — no Plaid, no bank login, no transaction reading. AllPaid has no account, no analytics, and no ad SDKs; data stays on-device with optional iCloud sync.
Does AllPaid have renewal reminders like Bobby?
Yes. AllPaid sends due-date and renewal notifications before a payment is due and surfaces upcoming items on a calendar and in home-screen and lock-screen widgets.
How much does AllPaid cost compared to Bobby?
AllPaid is free with an optional Pro tier from $1.99/month, $12.99/year, or $24.99 lifetime. Bobby is free for a limited number of subscriptions with a small one-time unlock for unlimited. Both avoid recurring finance-service subscriptions.
Can AllPaid track things that aren't subscriptions?
Yes — that's the main reason to choose it over Bobby. AllPaid handles one-off and irregular bills alongside recurring subscriptions, with due dates on a calendar rather than a subscription-only grid.
