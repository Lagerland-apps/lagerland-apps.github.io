App revenue dashboard · Native · Direct API

An Appfigures alternative
that reads from Apple, not a warehouse.

Appfigures is a capable SaaS dashboard for app sales, subscriptions, and rankings. It also stores your App Store Connect data on its servers. AppMeta Pulse takes a simpler path: it reads from App Store Connect's API directly, on your device, using your Apple ID. No middleman.

How does AppMeta Pulse compare to Appfigures?

A SaaS dashboard and a native client serve overlapping but different needs. Here's the line.

Where data lives
AppMeta Pulse: On your device. Fetched on demand from App Store Connect using your Apple ID.

Appfigures: Stored in their cloud after granting access, enabling cross-account analytics and historical caching.
Auth model
AppMeta Pulse: Your Apple ID, stored in iOS Keychain, talking to App Store Connect directly.

Appfigures: OAuth or API-key handoff to their servers, which then poll on your behalf.
Featureset
AppMeta Pulse: Read-only dashboard for sales, downloads, subscriptions, refunds, ranks. Pure visibility.

Appfigures: Broader — keyword tracking, competitor data, review monitoring, historical archives.
Pricing
AppMeta Pulse: From $0.99/month, on a per-user basis.

Appfigures: Tiered SaaS, scaling with apps and seats.

Who should pick AppMeta Pulse vs Appfigures?

Pick AppMeta Pulse if…
  • You're an indie or small team and the dashboard is for you, not a sales org
  • You don't want your revenue data sitting in a third-party warehouse
  • You check sales on your phone several times a day and want native widgets
  • You don't need competitor or keyword tracking
Stick with Appfigures if…
  • You need cross-store data (Google Play, Mac App Store, etc.) in one view
  • You track competitor keywords and historical app rankings
  • You share dashboards across a team and need seat-based access control
  • You want long-term historical caching beyond Apple's API window

Questions

FAQ

What is a good native alternative to Appfigures?
AppMeta Pulse is a native iPhone, iPad, and Mac alternative to Appfigures and similar dashboards. It uses your own Apple ID to read directly from App Store Connect's API, so your sales, downloads, subscriptions, and refunds never pass through a third-party data warehouse.
Does AppMeta Pulse store my App Store Connect data on a server?
No. AppMeta Pulse fetches data on-device, on demand, using your Apple ID credentials stored in iOS Keychain. Lagerland Apps never sees your revenue, downloads, or any business data.
How much does AppMeta Pulse cost compared to Appfigures?
AppMeta Pulse starts from $0.99/month for a single dashboard — a small monthly cost to cover App Store Connect API rate-limit infrastructure on the user's side. Appfigures and similar SaaS dashboards typically charge per-app or per-seat at higher tiers.
Does AppMeta Pulse support multiple developer accounts?
Yes. AppMeta Pulse supports switching between multiple App Store Connect accounts you have access to, useful for indie developers and small teams managing more than one team.
