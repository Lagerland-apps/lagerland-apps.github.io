How does RightSplit compare to Splitwise?

Different jobs. Splitwise tracks who owes whom over time; RightSplit settles one bill, fairly, right now.

Workflow
RightSplit: Scan receipt → detect items → assign per person → share result. One-and-done.

Splitwise: Add expense to a group → track running balance → settle later.
Item-level vs equal-split
RightSplit: Item-by-item by default; shared dishes split cleanly; tip and tax calculated per share.

Splitwise: Equal-split is default; itemising is manual entry.
Account requirement
RightSplit: None. Nobody in the group needs an account. See the Privacy Manifest.

Splitwise: Accounts required for sync; group members need an account to participate.
Ongoing balances
RightSplit: No. Built for one-off settles.

Splitwise: Yes — that's the core feature.

Who should pick RightSplit vs Splitwise?

Pick RightSplit if…
  • Your splits are mostly one-off (dinners out, weekend trips, group brunches)
  • The bills are itemised and equal-splitting is unfair
  • You don't want everyone in the group to need an account
  • You'd rather not have a running ledger of "who owes whom"
  • You care that the receipt image stays on your phone
Stick with Splitwise if…
  • You have ongoing shared expenses (rent, utilities, household items)
  • You and your group rely on the running-balance feature
  • You want multi-currency expense tracking across long-term groups
  • You actively use Splitwise Pro features (charts, receipts, OCR)

FAQ

What is the best Splitwise alternative for one-off bills?
RightSplit is a privacy-first iPhone alternative to Splitwise designed for one-off bill splitting at dinners and trips. You scan the receipt, RightSplit detects items on-device using Apple's Vision framework, you assign items per person with shared dishes and tip, then share the result via Messages or WhatsApp. No account, no group ledger, no ongoing IOUs.
Does RightSplit upload my receipt photos?
No. Receipt OCR runs entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework. The image and the parsed items never leave your phone.
Can RightSplit handle ongoing group balances like Splitwise?
No — by design. RightSplit is for one-off splits where you settle immediately. If you and your roommates have a running tab over months, Splitwise's ledger model is the right tool. RightSplit is for the dinner check, the trip dinner, the AirBnB grocery run.
Does RightSplit require accounts for everyone in the group?
No. Nobody needs an account. You enter names locally and share the result via the iOS share sheet — Messages, WhatsApp, or whatever app you choose.
