On-device AI · No account · Works with any vet

A Pawprint alternative
that reads the document instead of phoning your vet.

Pawprint — now Great Pet Care — built its reputation on fetching vaccination records from participating U.S. clinics into a cloud account. Pawza takes a different, more private path: photograph any vet document and on-device AI fills in the record for you. No clinic integration, no account, no server — which means it works with any vet in any country, and your data never leaves your device unless you sync it through your own iCloud. If you want records pulled automatically from a U.S. vet, Great Pet Care still does that; if you want a private record you build and own, Pawza is the alternative.

Questions

FAQ

Is Pawprint still available?
Pawprint has been rebranded as Great Pet Care and is still available and updated. It remains an account- and cloud-based app, historically known for fetching vaccination records from participating U.S. clinics. For a private, on-device replacement, Pawza is the closest alternative.
What is the best Pawprint alternative for iPhone?
Pawza, if you want a private on-device record. Instead of requesting records from your vet's system, Pawza has you photograph any document and on-device AI fills in the fields you confirm. It works with any vet, needs no account, and stores everything on your device with optional iCloud sync.
Can Pawza fetch records from my vet like Pawprint did?
No — that's the honest difference. Pawprint's retrieval depended on integrations with participating U.S. clinics. Pawza reads the documents you already have with on-device AI instead, so it works with any vet anywhere and keeps your data private, but you do the scanning rather than having records pulled automatically.
Is Pawza more private than Great Pet Care?
Yes, by design. Pawza has no account and no server of its own; records live on-device with optional private iCloud sync, and document scanning runs on-device. Great Pet Care is a cloud-account service. Pawza also has no ads or third-party tracking.
How much does Pawza cost?
Free for one pet; Pro is $2.99/month or $19.99/year (each with a 7-day free trial), or $39.99 once as a Lifetime purchase with Family Sharing. No ads.
