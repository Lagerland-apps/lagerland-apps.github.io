FAQ

What is the best TrackMySubs alternative for iPhone?
AllPaid is the closest native-iPhone alternative for people who don't want a web account. TrackMySubs is a browser-based service with logins; AllPaid keeps tracking on-device with no account, no web login, and no bank aggregation, and covers bills as well as subscriptions.
How is AllPaid different from TrackMySubs?
TrackMySubs is web-first SaaS with cross-platform access and folders, your data on their servers. AllPaid is a native iPhone app with on-device data, no account, plus one-off bills, a calendar, receipt detection, and widgets. The trade-off is web ubiquity versus on-device privacy and iPhone-native surfacing.
Does AllPaid store my data in the cloud like TrackMySubs?
No. AllPaid stores data on-device; optional iCloud sync uses your own private iCloud. There's no AllPaid server, no account, and no third-party analytics. TrackMySubs keeps data on its servers behind a login.
Does AllPaid send renewal reminders like TrackMySubs?
Yes. AllPaid sends due-date and renewal notifications and surfaces upcoming items on a calendar and in widgets, with no web dashboard to open.
How much does AllPaid cost compared to TrackMySubs?
AllPaid is free with optional Pro from $1.99/month, $12.99/year, or $24.99 lifetime. TrackMySubs has a free tier for limited subscriptions and paid plans above. AllPaid's lifetime option removes the recurring cost.
Can I use AllPaid without an internet connection?
Yes. As a native on-device app, AllPaid works offline. TrackMySubs, being web-based, needs a connection and login to reach your data.
