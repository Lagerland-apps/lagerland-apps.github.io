FAQ

What is the best Permute alternative for Mac?
MediaKit — the closest native iPhone and Mac alternative with a wider toolset. Permute is a polished AV-and-image converter; MediaKit keeps that on-device approach and extends to 152 tools that also cover PDF and archive work in one app.
Does MediaKit run on iPhone too, unlike Permute?
Yes. MediaKit is a Universal Purchase: buy it once and use it natively on both iPhone and Mac. Permute is a Mac-only desktop app, so MediaKit's on-device iPhone support is a genuine advantage if you also convert files on your phone.
Does MediaKit do everything Permute does?
For common cases, yes — drag-and-drop video (H.264, HEVC, AV1), audio, and image conversion, on-device. Permute is mature and focused; MediaKit's advantage is breadth, adding PDF and archive tools.
Is MediaKit on-device like Permute?
Yes — both run entirely on your device with no upload. MediaKit is engineered to avoid upload-to-a-website conversion workflows.
How does MediaKit's pricing compare to Permute?
MediaKit is free to start; Pro is $3.99/month, $22.99/year, or $39.99 lifetime. Permute is typically a one-time purchase (~$14.99) or via Setapp. AV-only? Permute's price is compelling. Want PDF and archives too? MediaKit's breadth wins.
Why pick MediaKit over Permute?
Breadth in one app — transcode a clip, compress a PDF, convert a HEIC, extract audio, unzip an archive, without switching tools. Permute is excellent but focused on AV and images.
Is MediaKit a modern native app?
Yes — a modern native iPhone and Mac app with drag-and-drop and a unified inspector, built for current iOS and macOS. Permute is also Mac-native and well-regarded for its clean design.
