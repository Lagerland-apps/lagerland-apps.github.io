How does AfterShift compare to Shift Worker?

We're not claiming AfterShift is "better." It's different — with different defaults. Here's where those differences matter.

How shifts get into the app
AfterShift: Reads your iOS Calendar and detects shifts automatically. No double-entry.

Shift Worker: Manual entry with pattern templates. Strong for fixed rotations.
Recovery + sleep model
AfterShift: Reads sleep from Apple Health, calculates recovery between shifts, suggests caffeine timing.

Shift Worker: Schedule-focused; sleep tracking is not the primary feature.
Privacy model
AfterShift: No account, no third-party SDKs, calendar and health data never leave the device. See the Privacy Manifest.

Shift Worker: Standard third-party analytics are typical for the category.
Pricing
AfterShift: Honest paid app, no subscription paywall on core shift detection.

Shift Worker: Freemium with a Pro subscription.

Who should pick AfterShift vs Shift Worker?

Pick AfterShift if…
  • Your shifts already live in a work calendar and you don't want to log them twice
  • You care more about recovery scoring than schedule rendering
  • You wear an Apple Watch and want HealthKit sleep data to drive the app
  • You refuse apps that require accounts or contain advertising SDKs
Stick with Shift Worker if…
  • You have years of pattern data in Shift Worker you don't want to migrate
  • Your rotation pattern is fixed and complex (5-on-5-off, 12-hour rotations) and the template engine is the feature you use
  • You don't want sleep/Health to factor in at all
  • Your crew shares schedules via Shift Worker-specific export

Questions

FAQ

What is the best alternative to Shift Worker for iPhone?
AfterShift is a privacy-first alternative built around two ideas: shifts you already keep in your work calendar should be auto-detected, and your phone already knows how you slept. It reads Calendar events and Apple Health sleep data, then surfaces recovery scores and caffeine timing without requiring an account or any data leaving your device.
Does AfterShift auto-detect shifts from the calendar?
Yes. AfterShift reads your iOS Calendar (with permission) to detect work shifts automatically, so you don't have to log a rotation twice. The detection runs on-device — no calendar contents are uploaded.
Does AfterShift work without an account?
Yes. AfterShift has no user accounts, no email sign-up, and no required login. You download it from the App Store and start using it — your data stays on the device, with optional iCloud sync through your own account.
Is AfterShift only for nurses?
No. AfterShift is designed for any rotating-schedule role: nurses, pilots, paramedics, factory operators, security and night-staff roles, and oil-and-gas crew rotations. If you have a non-fixed schedule, the recovery calculations apply.
