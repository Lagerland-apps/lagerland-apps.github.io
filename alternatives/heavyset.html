How does LiftLog compare to Heavyset?

Similar philosophy — different pricing and program depth.

Pricing
LiftLog: Free 7-day trial (no card), then pay-what-you-can one-time $9.99–$39.99.

Heavyset: Subscription for full features.
Programs & periodization
LiftLog: PPL, Upper/Lower, Full Body, Starting Strength, 5/3/1 with Training Max + linear / DUP / block / wave periodization and auto deloads.

Heavyset: Routine builder, minimal program templates.
Analytics
LiftLog: Estimated 1RM (Epley) + actual best 1/3/5-rep PRs, best volume, best set, 90-day heatmap, 30-day deltas.

Heavyset: Volume and rep tracking; less periodization-aware reporting.

LiftLog: No account, no third-party tracking, data on device + optional iCloud sync to your own Apple ID.

Heavyset: Account / sync model varies by version.
