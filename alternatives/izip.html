FAQ

What is the best iZip alternative for iPhone and Mac?
MediaKit, when you want more than zip and unzip. iZip is a popular free iOS utility that extracts ZIP, RAR, and 7Z and creates encrypted ZIPs. MediaKit also creates archives (ZIP, TAR, TAR.GZ, GZIP, XZ, BZIP2) and extracts RAR v4 and v5 with AES-256 and path-traversal-safe handling, then adds 140+ more on-device tools for video, audio, image, and PDF — one app, no ads, Universal Purchase for iPhone and Mac.
Does MediaKit handle zip and unzip like iZip?
Yes for the common jobs — MediaKit creates ZIP, TAR, TAR.GZ, GZIP, XZ, and BZIP2 and extracts RAR v4 and v5, with AES-256 via Apple's AppleArchive, path-traversal-safe extraction, and batch extraction. iZip extracts a longer list of legacy containers (7Z, ZIPX, ISO, CAB, DEB, RPM), so for those specific formats iZip remains the more complete extractor.
Is MediaKit on-device like iZip?
Yes — every MediaKit operation runs 100% on your device with no upload, no ads, and no account. iZip also processes archives locally, though its free tier shows ads and offers optional cloud-drive integrations.
How does MediaKit's pricing compare to iZip?
iZip is free with ads, plus a Premium IAP (around $7.99) that removes ads and unlocks AES and HEIC conversion. MediaKit is free to start with five tools free forever; Pro is $3.99/month, $22.99/year, or $39.99 lifetime, with no ads. Unzip-only? iZip's free tier is fine. Want archive creation plus 140+ other tools without ads? MediaKit's breadth wins.
Why pick MediaKit over iZip?
Breadth in one ad-free app — archive create and extract (RAR v4/v5, AES-256, path-traversal safe) plus 140+ other tools: compress a video, convert a HEIC, normalize audio, compress a PDF — all on-device, as a single Universal Purchase for iPhone and Mac. iZip is a focused, free zip utility.
Does MediaKit run on both iPhone and Mac?
Yes — one Universal Purchase covers iPhone (iOS 17+) and Mac (macOS 14+). iZip is primarily an iOS and iPadOS app from ComcSoft that also runs on Apple Silicon Macs and Apple Vision. Verified 2026-05-31 against the App Store; competitor pricing and features change — re-verify.
