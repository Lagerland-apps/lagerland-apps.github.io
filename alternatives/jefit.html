FAQ

What is the best Jefit alternative for iPhone?
GymLogger X — a fast, private logger without the social network, cloud account, ads, or subscription. It keeps 1,500+ exercises, smart programs, and plateau detection on-device, with Apple Watch logging and a one-time lifetime option.
Does GymLogger X have a community like Jefit?
No, intentionally. Jefit's community and shared routines are a real draw for people who want them; GymLogger X is for the lifter who wants a quiet, fast log with no account or feed. If the community is why you use Jefit, keep it.
Does GymLogger X show ads like Jefit's free tier?
No ads at any tier. Jefit's free tier is ad-supported with Elite to remove them; GymLogger X's free tier is ad-free, and Pro is a small subscription or a one-time lifetime purchase.
Does GymLogger X sync without a Jefit-style account?
Yes — it uses your own iCloud, not a service account. No login, no cloud profile, no analytics; data stays on your device and private iCloud.
How much does GymLogger X cost compared to Jefit?
GymLogger X is free at base; Pro is $1.99/month, $17.99/year, or $39.99 lifetime. Jefit is free with ads; Elite is around $6.99/month or $39.99/year. The lifetime tier ends the recurring cost.
Does GymLogger X log on Apple Watch?
Yes — first-class wrist logging of sets and reps between sets, no phone needed.
