Honest comparison

We're not going to claim GymLogger X is "better" than Strong. It's different — with different priorities. Here's where those differences matter.

Privacy model
GymLogger X: No account required. No third-party analytics. Data stays on device; iCloud sync uses your own account.

Strong: Account required for sync. Standard third-party analytics are typical for the category.
Apple Watch
GymLogger X: Logging from watch is a first-class feature — set/rep logging directly on-wrist.

Strong: Has Apple Watch support; feature parity has varied historically.
Pricing philosophy
GymLogger X: Free core + optional Pro. No subscription required for the basic logging you need day-to-day.

Strong: Freemium with a subscription for advanced features — premium costs scale per month or year.
Built by
GymLogger X: One independent Apple developer, part of a broader privacy-first catalogue.

Strong: A larger, dedicated workout-app team.

Who should pick what

Pick GymLogger X if…
  • You want Apple Watch logging as the primary interaction
  • You refuse app accounts on principle
  • You want a non-subscription option for core features
  • You care that your workout data never touches a third-party server
  • You're already invested in the Apple Health + Lagerland ecosystem
Stick with Strong if…
  • You have years of Strong workout history you don't want to migrate
  • You rely on Strong-specific community or export features
  • You specifically want Strong's UI and workflow
  • Your lifting group all uses Strong for shared routines

FAQ

What is the best alternative to Strong workout app?
GymLogger X is a fast, native iPhone and Apple Watch workout logger built as an alternative to subscription-locked trackers like Strong. It has 1,500+ exercises, Apple Watch logging, smart programs, and plateau detection — without requiring a recurring subscription for core features.
Does GymLogger X work on Apple Watch?
Yes. GymLogger X supports logging sets and reps directly from Apple Watch, making it practical to track workouts without pulling out your phone between sets.
Is GymLogger X free?
GymLogger X has a free tier and optional Pro features. Unlike subscription-only workout trackers, the core workout-logging functionality is accessible without a recurring payment.
Does GymLogger X track my personal data?
No. GymLogger X has no third-party tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no user account requirement. Workout data stays on your device and syncs through your own iCloud account if you enable it.
