Questions

FAQ

What is the best 81Dojo alternative for solo training?
Shogiful is the closest alternative for structured solo improvement: every game analyzed on-device by YaneuraOu, mistakes explained in plain language, and training generated from your own weaknesses with spaced repetition. Fully offline, no account, on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
How is Shogiful different from 81Dojo?
81Dojo is built around people — rated games, teaching games, community. Shogiful is built around the improvement loop — play, analyze, understand, train — with an engine and coaching layer that works on demand, every game, offline.
Is Shogiful good for beginners who can't read kanji?
Yes — it's specifically built for them. Western piece sets with movement indicators make the board readable from the first game, and kanji is introduced progressively as you train.
How much does Shogiful cost compared to 81Dojo?
Shogiful is free with all 10 opponents, 2 analyses/day, and 3 puzzles/day; Premium is $1.99/month, $9.99/year, or $19.99 lifetime. 81Dojo is free with optional supporter memberships.
Does Shogiful have teaching games like 81Dojo?
Not human ones. Instead: tiered AI opponents from true beginner to advanced, full analysis of every game with plain-language explanations, and training targeted at your detected weaknesses — available any time, no scheduling.
Can I use both 81Dojo and Shogiful?
Yes — they pair naturally. Play rated games on 81Dojo; analyze your habits, drill tsume, and fix recurring mistakes in Shogiful. KIF import from 81Dojo game records is under consideration on the roadmap.
