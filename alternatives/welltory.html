FAQ

Do I need an Apple Watch, or does Observa work with Oura, Garmin, and Fitbit?
No Apple Watch required. Observa reads Apple Health and interprets whatever is in it — Oura, Whoop, Garmin, or Fitbit data all work (Garmin/Fitbit usually via a sync app like Health Sync). Runs on iPhone and iPad.
What is the best Welltory alternative without an account?
Observa, for people who want interpretation without a cloud account or heavy subscription. It reads the HRV, RHR, and sleep already in your Apple Health — from any device that writes there — and explains which habits move each metric against your baseline — on-device, no account.
Is Observa more private than Welltory?
Yes — entirely on-device, no servers, no account, no analytics; App Privacy label lists zero data collected. Welltory syncs data to its cloud behind an account.
Does Observa measure HRV with the camera like Welltory?
No. Welltory's signature is camera HRV measurements; Observa interprets the HRV your Apple Watch already records. Want spot camera readings? Welltory. Want passive interpretation? Observa.
Does Observa avoid Welltory's subscription?
Observa's core is free, Pro from $3.99/month or $49.99 lifetime. Welltory is freemium with a subscription gating much of its analysis. The lifetime tier removes recurring cost.
What does Observa tell me that Welltory doesn't?
Which of your habits consistently move HRV, sleep efficiency, and energy against your 60-day baseline, with sample size and confidence — narrower and more interpretive than Welltory's dashboard, and fully on-device.
Can I use both Observa and Welltory?
Yes — take camera HRV readings in Welltory if you like, and use Observa to interpret the broader Apple Health picture passively and on-device.
