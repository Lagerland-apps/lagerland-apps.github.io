Ads

2048 (Ketchapp): Free with ads; one-time IAP to remove ads.

FAQ

What is the best ad-free alternative to 2048 by Ketchapp?
Zen 2048 is the closest like-for-like alternative for iPhone — identical rules, but no ads of any kind, plus a real 3D board, 17 themes, fully offline play, and no tracking. Free to play, with only optional theme packs and undo tokens.
Does Zen 2048 have the same rules as Ketchapp's 2048?
Yes, exactly. Swipe to slide all tiles, merge two equal numbers into their sum, a new tile spawns each move, reach 2048 and keep going. Nothing is relearned — only the look and feel change.
Is Zen 2048 really free if Ketchapp's 2048 is free?
Both are free downloads, but Ketchapp's is free because it's ad-supported (with an IAP to remove ads). Zen 2048 has no ads and no ad-removal upsell — its only purchases are optional themes ($1.99-$7.99) and undo tokens ($0.99-$6.99).
What does Zen 2048 add over the standard 2048?
A true 3D board you can rotate, 17 designer material themes, tuned haptics, a daily challenge with streaks, optional Game Center, and a strict no-ads, no-tracking, offline design.
Does Zen 2048 track me or need an account?
No. No account, no sign-up, no third-party ad or analytics SDKs, and a privacy manifest that declares no collected data. Everything stays on your device.
