FAQ

Is there a HabitNow app for iPhone?
HabitNow is Android-first on Google Play; there's no first-party iPhone version. On Apple, the closest calm equivalent is Taskful Day — a no-streak daily planner native on iPhone, iPad, and Mac with iCloud sync.
What is the best HabitNow alternative on iPhone?
Taskful Day. It covers the same daily-routine job in a calmer frame — no streaks, no guilt, no account, plus a native Mac app. The trade-off is that it deliberately omits HabitNow's heavier gamification and statistics in favour of single-day focus.
Does Taskful Day handle habits, tasks, and routines like HabitNow?
It's built around planning and finishing a realistic day, covering daily habits and tasks together. It avoids streak counters and elaborate stats — the focus is today, finished calmly.
Does Taskful Day require an account?
No. No account, no tracking, no ads. Data stays on-device and syncs via your own iCloud — a clean setup when migrating from Android.
Can I move my HabitNow data into Taskful Day?
There's no direct import. The practical migration is to recreate your few daily anchors by hand; since Taskful Day focuses on a realistic daily plan rather than long histories, it usually takes a few minutes.
How does Taskful Day's pricing compare to HabitNow?
Taskful Day is free with optional Pro from $2.99/month and a one-time lifetime option; the core flow stays free. HabitNow is free on Android with a one-time premium unlock.
