How does Pawza compare to 11pets?

Both keep your pets' health records in one place. Here's where they diverge.

Getting records in
Pawza: Snap a vet document and on-device AI fills in vaccine, date, medication, dose, and clinic — you confirm.

11pets: Manual entry across its forms, with photo attachments for documents.
Where data lives
Pawza: On your device, with optional sync to your own private iCloud. No account, no Pawza server.

11pets: In an 11pets cloud account, synced across devices and the web.
Pricing
Pawza: Free for one pet; Pro $2.99/mo, $19.99/yr, or $39.99 once (Lifetime, Family Sharing).

11pets: Free tier; premium subscription (commonly ~$4.99/mo). No one-time lifetime option.
Platforms
Pawza: iPhone and iPad (universal, one purchase). Apple-only.

11pets: iPhone, Android, and a web app — better if your household isn't all-Apple.
Breadth vs focus
Pawza: Focused on the owner's private medical record + AI scanning, reminders, weight, photos, and vet PDF.

11pets: A broad 50+ feature platform, including provider collaboration and tools for shelters and breeders.
Sharing with the vet
Pawza: One-tap vet-visit PDF (Pro) you hand over or email.

11pets: Granular, time-limited sharing with veterinary providers and other professionals.

Questions

FAQ

What is the best 11pets alternative for iPhone?
Pawza is the closest private, Apple-native alternative. Both track vaccinations, medications, weight, and vet visits for multiple pets, but Pawza scans vet documents with on-device AI and fills records in for you, and keeps your data on-device with no account. 11pets stores data in a cloud account and also runs on Android and the web.
Does Pawza require an account like 11pets?
No. Pawza has no account and no server of its own. Records stay on your device, and optional iCloud sync uses your own private iCloud through your Apple ID, compatible with Advanced Data Protection. 11pets uses an account to sync across iPhone, Android, and web and to share with providers.
How much does Pawza cost compared to 11pets?
Pawza is free for one pet; Pro is $2.99/month, $19.99/year (each with a 7-day free trial), or $39.99 once as a Lifetime purchase with Family Sharing. 11pets has a free tier and a premium subscription (commonly around $4.99/month). Pawza offers a one-time lifetime price; 11pets premium is subscription-only.
Can Pawza scan vet documents instead of manual entry?
Yes — it's Pawza's headline feature. Photograph a vaccination certificate, lab result, or prescription and Pawza reads it on-device and fills in the fields for you to confirm. Documents are never uploaded to a server. In 11pets these records are entered manually.
When should I choose 11pets over Pawza?
Choose 11pets if you need an Android or web app, if you're a breeder, shelter, groomer, or vet professional who needs its collaboration tools, or if you want its very broad 50+ feature set. Pawza is focused on the individual owner with private on-device records and AI scanning on iPhone and iPad.
