How does Zen 2048 compare to Threes?

Two different merge puzzles with a shared ancestor. Here's where each one fits.

The mechanic
Zen 2048: Merge two equal tiles into their double — 2+2=4, toward 2048 and beyond.

Threes: Merge 1+2 into 3, then equal multiples of three. A deliberately different, deeper system.
Pace & feel
Zen 2048: Fast, forgiving, calming — a five-minute game with no timer.

Threes: Slow, demanding, every move counts. A puzzle to study.
Presentation
Zen 2048: Real 3D board you can tilt and rotate, 17 designer material themes.

Threes: Beloved hand-drawn characters and a single signature art style.
Price
Zen 2048: Free, no ads; optional themes and undo tokens only.

Threes: Paid (with a free ad-supported tier historically — check the current listing).
Privacy & offline
Zen 2048: No account, no tracking, nothing collected; fully offline.

Threes: Standard mobile game; the free ad-supported builds include ad SDKs.
What it's for
Zen 2048: The calm, ad-free home for the classic 2048 mechanic.

Threes: A design landmark worth owning on its own terms.

FAQ

Is Zen 2048 the same as Threes?
No — different games. Threes merges 1+2 into 3, then equal multiples of three. 2048 merges two equal tiles into their double toward 2048. Threes is the original design; Zen 2048 is a polished, free, 3D take on the 2048 mechanic it inspired.
I want a free alternative to Threes — what should I get?
If you want a free, calm, ad-free sliding-merge puzzle and you're happy with the 2048 mechanic, Zen 2048 fits — free, 3D, 17 themes, no ads. If you specifically want the Threes mechanic, the honest answer is to buy Threes itself.
Which is harder, Threes or 2048?
Threes is the deeper, more demanding design — its board fills slowly and every move matters. 2048 is faster and more forgiving. Zen 2048 keeps that approachable pace and adds a no-pressure presentation with an optional undo.
Does Zen 2048 cost money like Threes?
Threes is paid (with a free ad-supported tier historically — check the current listing). Zen 2048 is free with no ads; only optional theme packs ($1.99-$7.99) and undo tokens ($0.99-$6.99) cost anything.
Can I play both?
Yes — they scratch different itches. Threes is the slow, deliberate classic worth owning; Zen 2048 is the quick, calming, ad-free 2048 for a five-minute window.
