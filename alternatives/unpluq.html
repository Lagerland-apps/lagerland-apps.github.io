When should I pick Unpluq instead?

Honest answer — pick Unpluq if any of these matter more than no-hardware, one-time pricing:

  • You want a physical tag as the unlock ritual, and you find the act of tapping (or deliberately not having the tag) more effective than any software gate.
  • You want the choice between a physical tag and several digital challenges to unlock.
  • You need cross-platform — Unpluq supports iOS and Android via NFC; EarnLock is iOS + Apple Watch only.
  • You don't want activity goals tied to movement.

EarnLock is for the person who doesn't want a tag to carry or a yearly bill to keep paying — who'd rather earn the unlock with a walk or a workout, enforced by Apple's Family Controls, proven by Apple Health, for $19.99 once.

How is EarnLock different from Unpluq?
Unpluq combines hardware and a subscription: a physical tag you tap (or a digital challenge) to unlock, with the app requiring an ongoing Premium plan. EarnLock is software-only and one-time priced: the gate is a daily activity goal your body meets, measured by Apple Health, and it unlocks automatically when you cross it. EarnLock has nothing to carry and no recurring fee, and surfaces the count on your Apple Watch.

Does EarnLock need a subscription or a tag?
Neither.
