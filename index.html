About Lagerland Apps

Lagerland Apps is an independent Apple developer building {{ live_apps.size }} privacy-first applications for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac. Every app is free of third-party tracking, has no advertising, and requires no account. Categories include health analytics, daily planning, bill tracking, workout logging, reading, and Apple developer tools. The apps are distributed through the Apple App Store under the Lagerland Apps developer brand.

Positioning: calm, focused software that respects the user's time and data. Monetization is honest — one-time purchases, optional Pro subscriptions, or freemium tiers. No dark patterns, no free trials that auto-charge, no growth hacks.

Target users: Apple users who refuse ad-supported apps, people who want local-first data, shift workers, indie developers, Apple Health power users, and anyone looking for single-purpose tools instead of bloated platforms.

Questions

Frequently asked

Who makes Lagerland Apps?
Lagerland Apps is an independent Apple developer building privacy-first apps for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac. Every app is designed, coded, and shipped by one person — no VC, no advertisers, no data brokers.
Do Lagerland apps track me or collect personal data?
No. Every app ships with zero third-party tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no analytics that identify users. Data stays on-device wherever possible, and any cloud sync uses your own iCloud account.
Do the apps require accounts or a login?
No. None of the apps require an account or email sign-up. You download from the App Store and start using them — nothing to register, nothing to lose access to.
How do you make money if there are no ads?
Through honest paid apps: one-time purchases, optional Pro subscriptions, and freemium models where the core experience is free. The apps work for you, not for advertisers.
Do the apps work on iPhone, iPad, and Mac?
Most apps target iPhone and iPad, several include Apple Watch support, and developer tools like AppMeta and ScreenFlow Studio are native Mac apps. Each app page lists its supported platforms.
What are the best alternatives to mainstream tracker-heavy apps?
Lagerland Apps offers privacy-first alternatives across categories: Taskful Day (now native on iPhone, iPad, and Mac) instead of feed-based planners, GymLogger X instead of subscription-locked workout loggers, Observa as a private Apple Health analytics alternative, and AllPaid as a calm bill tracker alternative to ad-supported finance apps.
