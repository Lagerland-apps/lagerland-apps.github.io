Questions

Frequently asked

Who makes Lagerland Apps?
Lagerland Apps is a one-person, independent Apple studio based in Finland, shipping privacy-first apps for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac since 2025. Every app is designed, coded, and supported by the same developer — no VC, no advertisers, no data brokers. Built under EU jurisdiction with GDPR as the default, not a compliance afterthought.
Do Lagerland apps track me or collect personal data?
No. Every app passes Apple's App Tracking Transparency check with no IDFA request, ships zero advertising SDKs, and includes no analytics that identify users. Data stays on-device wherever possible, any cloud sync uses your own iCloud account (compatible with Advanced Data Protection), and you can verify the claim on each app's App Store Privacy Nutrition Label.
Do the apps require accounts or a login?
No. None of the apps require an account or email sign-up. You download from the App Store and start using them — nothing to register, nothing to lose access to.
How do you make money if there are no ads?
Through honest paid apps: one-time purchases, optional Pro subscriptions, and freemium models where the core experience is free. The apps work for you, not for advertisers.
Do the apps work on iPhone, iPad, and Mac?
Most apps target iPhone and iPad, several include Apple Watch support, and developer tools like AppMeta and ScreenFlow Studio are native Mac apps. Each app page lists its supported platforms.
What are the best alternatives to mainstream tracker-heavy apps?
Lagerland Apps offers privacy-first alternatives across categories: Taskful Day (now native on iPhone, iPad, and Mac) instead of feed-based planners, GymLogger X instead of subscription-locked workout loggers, Observa as a private Apple Health analytics alternative, and AllPaid as a calm bill tracker alternative to ad-supported finance apps.
