Short, honest guides that take a specific frustration ("I want a workout app without paying monthly forever") and point at a privacy-first Apple-native answer — without hiding the trade-offs.

"I want a workout app without paying monthly forever."
Strong, Hevy, and Fitbod are all subscription-first. GymLogger X offers a free tier and a $44.99 one-time lifetime option instead.
"I want to track bills without linking my bank account."
Rocket Money and Truebill require Plaid-based bank access. AllPaid is manual-first, fully private, and has no aggregator.
