The ads problem, answered honestly

If you've tried to find a clean 2048 on the App Store and given up, it wasn't you. 2048's rules were open-sourced in 2014, so the store filled with clones — and because the puzzle is simple and free to copy, almost all of them monetise the only way a copied game can: ads. Pre-roll video before a game, interstitials every few moves, lives you refill by watching, and reward loops bolted onto a number puzzle. The Ketchapp version is the cleanest of the mass-market free ones and still shows banners and interstitials; 2048 Cube Winner goes much further. Zen 2048 (ours) takes the opposite route: it's free with no ads at all and no tracking — funded only by optional designer themes and undo tokens you never need — and it adds the things ads crowd out, a real 3D board and 17 material themes. The design and engineering story is in our journal post on rebuilding 2048 in 3D. Whichever you pick: a number puzzle this small should never cost you a video ad per game.

FAQ

What is the best 2048 app for iPhone?
It depends on the job. Zen 2048 (ours) is the best ad-free version — classic 2048 rebuilt as a real 3D board with 17 themes, no ads, no tracking. The Ketchapp version is the most familiar free one but is ad-supported. The original by Gabriele Cirulli is the free open-source web game. Threes is a different, paid, excellent puzzle.
What is the best 2048 game without ads?
Zen 2048 — no banners, no interstitials, no rewarded video, no "watch to continue." It's free with no ad-supported tier, funded only by optional themes and undo tokens, and runs fully offline with no tracking SDKs.
Is 2048 the same as Threes?
No — different games, and Threes came first. Threes merges 1+2 into 3, then equal multiples of three; it's a slower, deeper, paid puzzle. 2048 merges two equal tiles into their double toward 2048. Want the Threes mechanic? Buy Threes. Want clean ad-free 2048? Zen 2048.
What is the original 2048, and can I still play it?
The original by Gabriele Cirulli (2014, MIT-licensed) is still free in a browser at play2048.co — canonical but a flat web page with no real offline mode. Zen 2048 keeps its exact rules in a native iPhone app with a 3D board, themes, and offline play.
Is there a 3D 2048 game?
Yes — Zen 2048 renders the board in real 3D you can tilt and spin to any angle, then it snaps back to a clean view to play. (2048 Cube Winner markets a 3D look too, but it's fixed and wrapped in heavy ads and reward loops.)
What is the best free 2048 game for iPhone?
Zen 2048 is the most polished free option and the only one that's free and ad-free. The Ketchapp version and the original are also free — the Ketchapp one is ad-supported, the original is the open-source web game.
