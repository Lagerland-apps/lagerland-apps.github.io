TheWait is a micro-mindfulness app for iPhone that helps people feel calmer during everyday waiting — queues, delays, appointments, commutes, and in-between moments. It provides gentle timers, breathing prompts, and grounding exercises designed for real-world durations (seconds to minutes, not hour-long sessions). One-tap start with no setup or onboarding. Customizable duration, style, and tone. Minimal, low-cognitive-load interface. Not a meditation course or habit tracker — specifically built for the stressful moments most mindfulness apps ignore. No ads, no tracking, no account required.
