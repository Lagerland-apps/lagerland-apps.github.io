TheWait is a beautiful event countdown app for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision Pro. It transforms waiting for important life moments — weddings, birthdays, trips, reunions, graduations, and personal milestones — into calm, visually stunning countdown experiences. Features include customizable themes with icons, colors, and photo backgrounds; Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets; gentle reminder notifications; multiple time display formats (days, weeks, exact time); and daily milestone notes for journaling the anticipation. Designed as a presence tool with soft gradients, large calming numbers, and minimal interface — no gamification, no streaks, no pressure. No ads, no tracking, no account required. Free with Premium from $1.99/month or $24.99 lifetime.
