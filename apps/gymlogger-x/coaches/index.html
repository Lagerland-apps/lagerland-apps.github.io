The problem coaches quietly deal with

PDF friction
Great programs lose momentum because delivery is clumsy: downloads, folders, instructions, and clients unsure what to do today.
Unnecessary messages
“Where do I find the workout?” “Which week am I on?” Not coaching — just friction caused by the format.
Overbuilt platforms
Many coaching apps force dashboards, check-ins, analytics, and admin work you never asked for — and don’t want.
Low follow-through
When starting feels complicated, clients delay. When they delay, they drop off — even with a good program.

Coach FAQ

Is this a full coaching platform?
No. GymLogger X intentionally avoids dashboards, client management, check-ins, and messaging. It focuses on program delivery and logging only.
Can I track my clients inside GymLogger X?
No. Clients use the app independently. This keeps your workload low and the experience focused.
Do clients have to pay?
GymLogger X is free to use with optional paid features. Clients decide if they want Pro — you don’t need to sell it.
Does this replace PDFs completely?
Yes — that’s the point. Programs live inside the app, not as files clients forget or lose.
Can I stop recommending it later?
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Recommend it only as long as it fits your coaching.
