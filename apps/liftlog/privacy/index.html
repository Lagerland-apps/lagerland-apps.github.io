--- layout: privacy title: "Privacy Policy" app_name: "LiftLog" overview: > LiftLog is a design-led strength training log for iPhone. Privacy is a core principle of the app and is treated with the same care as the rest of its design. This policy explains what information LiftLog processes, how it is used, and the choices you have. data_collection: > LiftLog processes the information you choose to enter into the app, such as workouts, exercises, sets, repetitions, weights, training notes, programs, and preferences. LiftLog does not collect or transmit personal data to external servers for advertising or tracking purposes. data_storage: > By default, all workout data and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account and synced across your devices using Apple’s infrastructure. LiftLog does not operate its own servers for storing workout data, and Lagerland Apps never receives a copy. third_parties: > LiftLog does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Apple processes limited data as part of services you choose to use, such as iCloud sync, App Store subscriptions, and local notifications. sensitive_data: > If you choose to connect Apple Health, LiftLog may write workout activity and active energy through Apple’s HealthKit framework, and may read workout-related data to keep your training history consistent with Apple Health. Health data is used only to provide workout logging, summaries, and progress views inside the app. LiftLog does not sell, share, or use health data for advertising purposes. children: > LiftLog is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. changes: > We may update this privacy policy from time to time.
