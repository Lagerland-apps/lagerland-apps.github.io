Local-currency equivalents are determined by the App Store. Prices may be adjusted by Apple per their currency-tier rules; the in-app paywall always shows the price you will be charged before you confirm.

Auto-renewal (Monthly and Yearly): Your subscription renews automatically at the end of each billing period unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the period ends. Renewals are charged to the payment method on file with your Apple ID. Manage or cancel at iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions at any time. Cancelling a current subscription ends auto-renewal but does not retroactively revoke access for the period you have already paid for.

7-day free trial (Monthly and Yearly): When you start a Monthly or Yearly subscription for the first time, Apple grants a 7-day free trial. If you cancel during the trial, you are not charged. If you do not cancel, you are charged the full Monthly or Yearly price at the end of the trial and on each renewal thereafter. Apple's one-trial-per-subscription-group rule applies — a user who has already used the trial on Monthly will not get a second trial on Yearly (or vice versa).

Family Sharing: Every paid tier (Monthly, Yearly, and Lifetime) is Family Sharing eligible. The family organizer purchases once, then enables sharing for EarnLock in iOS Settings → your name → Family Sharing → Subscriptions / Purchases. Each family member (up to 5) sees EarnLock as available in their App Store account and can download it on their own devices at no extra cost. Family Sharing handles the billing entitlement only; activity data, blocked-app selections, and streak history remain local to each family member's device.

Lifetime: A single non-consumable purchase ($19.99) that grants Premium for as long as Apple supports the App Store account that bought it. There is no recurring charge, no auto-renewal, and no scheduled price change. If the Lifetime price ever changes in the future, the new price will be visible on the App Store listing and in the in-app paywall before you confirm — existing Lifetime owners are never re-billed.

Restore Purchases: If you change devices or reinstall the app, tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall. EarnLock asks Apple for your current entitlements and updates locally.

We have no insight into the outcome of your request and cannot expedite, escalate, or supplement it.
