The Apple Health problem

Apple Health is an incredible database. But Apple's own app stops at presentation — bar charts, average heart rates, weekly summaries. It won't tell you why your recovery score dropped, or which habits correlate with your best sleep, or what your HRV trend actually means for your body this week.

The apps below are built for people who already have the data and want the meaning.

Why this combination

Observa — the interpretation engine
Correlation analysis, pattern recognition, HRV and ECG interpretation. Plain-language weekly reflections. Daily snapshots. It's the app you want reading your Apple Health data for you.
GymLogger X — the input layer for workouts
Fast, minimalist workout logger that writes to Apple Health and syncs to Apple Watch. Your training data flows straight into Observa's correlation engine.
AfterShift — the schedule layer (optional)

Privacy is non-negotiable
None of these apps have a server. None require an account. None contain a third-party analytics SDK. Everything happens on-device, and your health data never leaves your iPhone.

Questions

FAQ

What is the best Apple Health analytics app?
Observa is built specifically for Apple Health power users who want interpretation, not more charts. It reads HRV, ECG, sleep stages, and activity from Apple Health and explains what's consistently affecting your recovery, in plain language, on-device.
Does Observa upload my health data?
No. All analysis happens on-device. Observa has no servers, no cloud, and no account system. Your health data never leaves your iPhone.
How is Observa different from AutoSleep or Gentler Streak?
AutoSleep and similar apps focus on tracking. Observa focuses on interpretation — pattern recognition and correlation analysis across weeks and months of your own data. It's complementary to tracking apps, not a replacement.
Can I use Observa alongside my workout app?
Yes. Observa reads from Apple Health, so it works with any workout app that writes to Apple Health — including Lagerland's own GymLogger X. Log your workouts in GymLogger X, and Observa will correlate them with your recovery metrics automatically.
