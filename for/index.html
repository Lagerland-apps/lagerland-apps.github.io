Four short pages that match a small, privacy-first app stack to the way you actually live and work — with the trade-offs stated out loud.

For shift workers
Nurses, first responders, and rotating-schedule workers: track sleep around shifts, understand recovery, plan calm days — all on-device.
For Apple Health power users
You already collect HRV, sleep, and activity data on Apple Watch. These apps turn it into plain-language answers — without uploading a single byte.
For indie iOS developers
Native Mac apps for App Store Connect metadata, revenue dashboards, and App Store screenshots — no browser tabs, credentials stay in your Keychain.
For calm productivity
A streak-free, ad-free planning stack: realistic days, calm reading, private countdowns. No flame counters, no guilt mechanics.
