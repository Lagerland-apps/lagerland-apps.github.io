Questions

FAQ

Is there a native Mac app for App Store Connect?
Yes — AppMeta is a native Mac client for App Store Connect that lets you edit metadata, localizations, IAPs, and version info locally, without using the web UI. Credentials are stored in Keychain and requests go directly to the App Store Connect API.
How do I monitor App Store revenue and subscriptions on iPhone?
AppMeta Pulse is a read-only iPhone companion for App Store Connect that shows revenue, subscription metrics, and a multi-app dashboard — designed for indie developers shipping multiple apps who don't want to open the App Store Connect website every day.
What's the fastest way to generate localised App Store screenshots?
ScreenFlow Studio is a native Mac app for batch-generating localised App Store screenshots. It supports bulk language import, Apple device frames, and batch export in every required App Store size.
Are these tools a replacement for Fastlane?
They complement it. Fastlane is a CLI automation toolchain; these are native GUI apps for the everyday editing, monitoring, and screenshot workflows most indie developers touch manually. Use them side-by-side.
